Vandelay Financial Corporation - Loan Products Guide

MORTGAGE LOANS

Fixed Rate Mortgage (30-Year):
- Current rate: 6.75% APR
- Loan amounts: $100,000 - $2,000,000
- Down payment: Minimum 3% for first-time buyers, 10% standard
- Monthly payment example: $1,297 per $200,000 borrowed
- Best for: Buyers who want predictable payments long-term
- Closing costs: Typically 2-5% of loan amount

Fixed Rate Mortgage (15-Year):
- Current rate: 6.00% APR
- Loan amounts: $50,000 - $1,500,000
- Down payment: Minimum 10%
- Monthly payment example: $1,688 per $200,000 borrowed
- Best for: Buyers who can afford higher payments and want to save on interest
- Total interest savings: Up to 50% compared to 30-year term

Adjustable Rate Mortgage (5/1 ARM):
- Initial rate: 5.50% APR (fixed for 5 years)
- Adjusts annually after initial period based on SOFR index
- Rate caps: 2% per year, 5% lifetime
- Best for: Buyers planning to move or refinance within 5 years

FHA Loan:
- Rate: 6.50% APR
- Down payment: As low as 3.5%
- Credit score: Minimum 580
- Mortgage insurance required (MIP)
- Best for: First-time buyers with limited savings

HOME EQUITY PRODUCTS

Home Equity Loan (HELOAN):
- Rates: 7.50% - 9.50% APR based on credit and LTV
- Borrow up to 85% of home equity
- Fixed monthly payments
- Terms: 5, 10, 15, or 20 years
- Interest may be tax-deductible (consult tax advisor)
- Best for: Large one-time expenses like renovations

Home Equity Line of Credit (HELOC):
- Rates: Prime + 0.5% to Prime + 2% (currently 8.50% - 10.00%)
- Variable rate with interest-only payment option
- Draw period: 10 years
- Repayment period: 20 years
- Credit limit: Up to 85% of home equity
- Best for: Ongoing expenses or projects with variable costs

PERSONAL LOANS

Unsecured Personal Loan:
- Rates: 8.99% - 21.99% APR (based on credit score)
- Amounts: $2,500 - $50,000
- Terms: 24-84 months
- No collateral required
- Funds deposited in 1-3 business days after approval
- Best for: Debt consolidation, home improvements, major purchases

Secured Personal Loan:
- Rates: 6.99% - 14.99% APR
- Secured by savings account or CD
- Lower rates than unsecured options
- Can help build credit history
- Best for: Building credit or getting better rates with collateral

AUTO LOANS

New Car Loan:
- Rates: 5.99% - 11.99% APR based on credit
- Terms: 36, 48, 60, or 72 months
- Up to 100% financing available
- Pre-approval available online
- Best for: Purchasing new vehicles

Used Car Loan:
- Rates: 6.99% - 13.99% APR
- Terms: 36-60 months
- Vehicles up to 7 years old, under 100,000 miles
- Best for: Purchasing quality used vehicles

BUSINESS LOANS

Small Business Term Loan:
- Rates: 8.25% - 12.25% APR
- Amounts: $50,000 - $5,000,000
- Terms: 1-7 years
- Requires 2+ years in business, $250K+ annual revenue
- Collateral may be required for larger amounts
- Best for: Equipment, expansion, working capital

SBA 7(a) Loan:
- Rates: Prime + 2.25% to Prime + 4.75%
- Amounts: Up to $5,000,000
- Terms: Up to 25 years for real estate
- SBA guarantee reduces risk
- Best for: Businesses that don't qualify for conventional loans

Business Line of Credit:
- Rates: Prime + 1% to Prime + 5%
- Credit limits: $25,000 - $500,000
- Draw and repay as needed
- Annual review for renewal
- Best for: Managing cash flow, seasonal inventory

LOAN APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS

For personal loans, you'll need:
1. Proof of income (recent pay stubs, W-2s, or tax returns)
2. Employment verification (employer contact or letter)
3. Government-issued ID
4. Social Security Number
5. Proof of residence

For business loans, additional requirements:
1. Business tax returns (2 years)
2. Business financial statements
3. Business plan (for startups or SBA loans)
4. Personal guarantee from owners with 20%+ ownership
