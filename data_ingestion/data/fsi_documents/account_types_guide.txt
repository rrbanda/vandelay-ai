Acme Bank Account Types Guide

CHECKING ACCOUNTS

Basic Checking:
- Monthly fee: $12 (waived with $500 minimum balance)
- Free debit card and online banking
- No interest earned
- Unlimited transactions
- Best for: Everyday banking needs

Premium Checking:
- Monthly fee: $25 (waived with $5,000 minimum balance)
- 0.05% APY interest
- Free cashier's checks and wire transfers
- Priority customer service
- Best for: Higher balance customers who want perks

Student Checking:
- No monthly fee (ages 17-24)
- Free debit card
- No minimum balance required
- Overdraft protection available
- Best for: College students and young adults

SAVINGS ACCOUNTS

Regular Savings:
- 0.50% APY interest
- $300 minimum opening deposit
- 6 free withdrawals per month
- FDIC insured up to $250,000
- Best for: Building an emergency fund

High-Yield Savings:
- 4.25% APY interest (promotional rate)
- $1,000 minimum balance
- Online access only
- Best for: Maximizing savings growth

Money Market Account:
- 3.50% APY interest
- $2,500 minimum balance
- Check writing privileges
- Tiered interest rates
- Best for: Higher balances with access needs

CERTIFICATES OF DEPOSIT (CDs)

6-Month CD: 4.00% APY, $1,000 minimum
12-Month CD: 4.50% APY, $1,000 minimum
24-Month CD: 4.75% APY, $1,000 minimum
60-Month CD: 5.00% APY, $1,000 minimum

Early withdrawal penalty: 90 days of interest for CDs under 12 months, 180 days for longer terms.

HOW TO OPEN AN ACCOUNT

Required Documents:
1. Government-issued photo ID (driver's license or passport)
2. Social Security Number
3. Proof of address (utility bill or lease)
4. Initial deposit

You can open an account:
- Online at acmebank.com (takes 10 minutes)
- At any branch location
- By phone at 1-800-ACME-BANK

ACCOUNT PROTECTION

All accounts are FDIC insured up to $250,000 per depositor. For joint accounts, coverage extends to $500,000.

Fraud protection includes:
- Zero liability for unauthorized transactions
- Real-time fraud alerts via text/email
- Card lock feature in mobile app
- 24/7 fraud monitoring
