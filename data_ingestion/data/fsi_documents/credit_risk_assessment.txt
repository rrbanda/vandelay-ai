Overview:

Risk Mitigation Strategies:

For Residential Mortgage Portfolio:
1. Maintain strict underwriting standards (max 43% DTI, min 620 credit score)
2. Require PMI for loans above 80% LTV
3. Geographic diversification targets (no state > 25% of portfolio)
4. Interest rate hedging through derivatives
5. Increase loan loss reserves to 2.5% of portfolio value
6. Early intervention program for borrowers showing stress signals

For Commercial Lending Portfolio:
1. Industry concentration limits (no sector > 35%)
2. Minimum DSCR requirement of 1.25x
3. Annual financial statement review for loans > $500,000
4. Personal guarantees required for small business loans
5. Collateral requirements for equipment and real estate loans
6. Covenant monitoring and early warning triggers

For High-Risk Customers:
1. Enhanced transaction monitoring (real-time for flagged accounts)
2. Quarterly KYC refresh and beneficial ownership verification
3. Automatic escalation for transactions > $50,000
4. SAR filing within 30 days of suspicious activity identification
5. Consider relationship termination for repeated violations

Credit Risk Limits (Vandelay Policy):
- Single borrower exposure: Maximum 10% of Tier 1 capital
- Industry concentration: Maximum 25% of total credit exposure
- Geographic concentration: Maximum 25% per state
- Product concentration: Maximum 40% in any single product type
- High-risk customer exposure: Maximum 5% of total deposits

Reporting Requirements:
Credit risk reports must be submitted to:
- Credit Risk Committee: Weekly
- Executive Risk Committee: Monthly
- Board Risk Committee: Quarterly
- OCC/Federal Reserve: Quarterly (Call Report)
- External Auditors: Annually
