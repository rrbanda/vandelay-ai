Vandelay Financial Corporation - Frequently Asked Questions

ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT

Q: How do I open a bank account?
A: You can open an account online at vandelayfinancial.com in about 10 minutes, visit any branch location, or call 1-800-VANDELAY. You'll need a government-issued ID, Social Security Number, and an initial deposit.

Q: What's the minimum balance to avoid fees?
A: For Basic Checking, maintain $500 to waive the $12 monthly fee. Premium Checking requires $5,000 to waive the $25 fee. Student Checking has no minimum balance or monthly fee.

Q: How do I set up direct deposit?
A: Provide your employer with our routing number (021000089) and your account number. Direct deposits typically start within 1-2 pay cycles.

Q: Can I have multiple checking accounts?
A: Yes! Many customers have separate accounts for bills, savings goals, and everyday spending. There's no limit to the number of accounts you can open.

ONLINE & MOBILE BANKING

Q: How do I reset my online banking password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, verify your identity with security questions or a text code, then create a new password.

Q: Is mobile check deposit safe?
A: Yes, mobile deposits are encrypted and secure. Simply endorse the check "For mobile deposit only" and snap photos of both sides in our app.

Q: What's the mobile deposit limit?
A: Standard accounts: $5,000 per day, $25,000 per month. Premium accounts: $10,000 per day, $50,000 per month.

Q: How do I send money to friends and family?
A: Use Zelle in our mobile app for instant transfers to anyone with a U.S. bank account. Just enter their email or phone number.

DEBIT CARDS & ATMs

Q: Where can I use my debit card without fees?
A: Free ATM access at 40,000+ ATMs nationwide in our network. International ATMs charge $3 per transaction plus any foreign fees.

Q: My debit card was lost/stolen. What do I do?
A: Immediately lock your card in the mobile app or call 1-800-VANDELAY. We'll send a replacement card within 5-7 business days, or expedite for $25.

Q: What's my daily ATM withdrawal limit?
A: Standard limit is $500 per day. You can request a temporary increase for travel or large purchases by calling customer service.

TRANSFERS & PAYMENTS

Q: How long do transfers take?
A: Internal transfers: Instant. External bank transfers: 1-3 business days. Wire transfers: Same day if submitted before 4 PM ET.

Q: What's the wire transfer fee?
A: Domestic incoming: Free. Domestic outgoing: $25. International incoming: $15. International outgoing: $45.

Q: How do I set up automatic bill pay?
A: In online banking, go to "Bill Pay" and add your billers. You can schedule one-time or recurring payments.

LOANS & CREDIT

Q: How do I check my loan balance?
A: Log in to online banking and select your loan account to see current balance, payment history, and payoff amount.

Q: Can I make extra payments on my mortgage?
A: Yes! Extra payments go directly to principal, reducing your total interest. There are no prepayment penalties on Vandelay mortgages.

Q: How do I apply for a loan?
A: Apply online, by phone, or in-branch. Have your income documentation, employment info, and identification ready. Many loan decisions are instant.

Q: What credit score do I need for a mortgage?
A: Minimum 580 for FHA loans, 620 for conventional loans. Higher scores get better rates. Check your score free in our mobile app.

SECURITY & FRAUD

Q: How do I report a suspicious transaction?
A: Call our 24/7 fraud hotline at 1-800-VANDELAY-FRAUD immediately. We'll investigate and issue a provisional credit within 10 business days.

Q: What is two-factor authentication?
A: An extra security layer that requires both your password and a code sent to your phone. Enable it in Settings > Security in online banking.

Q: How do I set up fraud alerts?
A: In the mobile app, go to Settings > Alerts and enable "Transaction Alerts" to get instant notifications for all purchases.

Q: Is my money insured?
A: Yes, all deposits are FDIC insured up to $250,000 per depositor. Joint accounts are insured up to $500,000.

FEES & CHARGES

Q: What happens if I overdraft my account?
A: We'll cover transactions up to $500 if you're enrolled in overdraft protection. Fee is $35 per overdraft, maximum 3 per day.

Q: How do I avoid overdraft fees?
A: Link a savings account for automatic transfers, set up low balance alerts, or enroll in overdraft protection from your credit line.

Q: Are there fees for paper statements?
A: Paper statements are $3/month. Switch to e-statements (free) in online banking under Settings > Statement Delivery.

CONTACT & SUPPORT

Q: What are branch hours?
A: Most branches are open Monday-Friday 9 AM-5 PM, Saturday 9 AM-1 PM. Some locations have extended hours.

Q: How do I schedule an appointment?
A: Book online at vandelayfinancial.com/appointments or call your local branch. We recommend appointments for mortgages, investments, and business accounts.

Q: How do I file a complaint?
A: Contact customer service at 1-800-VANDELAY or visit our website. We respond to all complaints within 5 business days.
