UNDERSTANDING YOUR CREDIT CARD

APR (Annual Percentage Rate):
- Purchase APR: Interest on purchases
- Balance transfer APR: Interest on transferred balances
- Cash advance APR: Higher rate for cash withdrawals

Credit Limit:
- Maximum amount you can charge
- Using less than 30% helps your credit score

Minimum Payment:
- Smallest amount due each month
- Paying only minimum means paying more interest

Grace Period:
- 21-25 days to pay without interest
- Only applies if you pay full balance each month
